About Me

Hi, I’m Taima Traore, a computer science student passionate about backend development, network security, building tools that make systems more efficient, and secure and simplifying complex workflows through automation and web development. I’ve worked on everything from protocol testing dashboards to secure APIs, and recently interned at UPS, where I contributed to real-world infrastructure and security initiatives. Outside of code, I serve as the President of my university’s NSBE chapter, where I lead initiatives to support and empower Black engineers in tech. I’m deeply interested in how technology can be used to create access and equity. When I’m not debugging or building something new, you’ll find me reading fiction, playing volleyball, or thinking about the next problem I want to solve.
